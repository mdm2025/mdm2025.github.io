Arrivals

John Wayne Airport (SNA)

John Wayne Airport is an international commercial and general aviation airport that serves Orange County and the Greater Los Angeles area. For domestic arrivals, SNA is the most convenient airport which is only 4.3 miles(6.9km) away from the UCI campus.

Los Angeles International Airport (LAX)

As the largest and busiest international airport on the West Coast of the United States, LAX is well-connected to most of the countries in the world. It's about 43 miles(≈69km) from the conference site. To get to UCI campus, taking a taxi/Uber is the most efficient way.

Parking Information

A valid parking permit is required at all times when parking on campus. Visitors may purchase a permit at any information kiosk, dispenser, or through UCI’s ParkbyPlate app located in select parking areas. Physical permits must be displayed.

Visit the UCI Transportation and Distribution Services website for additional information on permits and where you can park.

Important for guests: Please make sure to park in stalls, in designated visitor parking lots, allocated to your permit type. General parking passes work for unmarked stalls. Reserved parking passes work for AR Reserved marked stalls. Stalls are typically marked with paint or posted signs; please be aware of all signs in lots.

Self Service

The closest parking lots to ISEB are 12B, 16, and 16H; these lots are self service only, no parking attendant is available. The permit dispenser is located at the entrance of Lot 16 and 16H, on Michael Drake Dr.

Lot 12B: This lot is directly behind ISEB, however only Reserved permits are allowed.

Lot 16: General and Reserved permits work here. Note that AR reserved marked stalls are located toward the front end of this lot.

Lot 16H: General permits or ParkbyPlate app work here.

Anteater Parking Structure

The closest parking lot to ISEB with a parking attendant/information kiosk is at Anteater Parking Structure.

If parking lots are full at 12B, 16, and 16H, you may also park at the Anteater Parking Structure and take a 15-min walk to ISEB.

Anteater Parking Structure has an attendant at the information kiosk located through the Anteater Drive & Peltason Drive intersection entrance.

Local Attractions


  • Amusement and Theme Parks
    • Disneyland Park: The world-famous theme park featuring classic attractions, beloved Disney characters, thrilling rides, and magical experiences for visitors of all ages.
    • Disney California Adventure Park: A dynamic theme park that celebrates the spirit of California with exciting rides, immersive lands, and entertainment inspired by Pixar, Marvel, and more.
    • Knott's Berry Farm: A historic amusement park known for its thrilling roller coasters, immersive Western-themed attractions, live entertainment, and delicious boysenberry treats.
  • Shopping and Entertainment
    • Irvine Spectrum Center: A vibrant outdoor shopping mall featuring over 130 stores, a giant Ferris wheel, a carousel, world-class dining, and the renowned Improv Comedy Club.
    • Downtown Disney District: A lively shopping and entertainment district located adjacent to Disneyland, offering unique boutiques, upscale dining, and live music performances.
    • Fashion Island: A luxury open-air shopping destination in Newport Beach, featuring high-end boutiques, designer stores, gourmet dining, and stunning Pacific Ocean views.
  • Museums and Cultural Centers
    • Pretend City Children's Museum: An interactive museum designed as a miniature city where children can engage in hands-on role-playing and educational activities.
    • Irvine Barclay Theatre: A premier performing arts venue that hosts a variety of cultural events, including Broadway shows, dance performances, and live music concerts.
  • Natural Attractions
    • San Joaquin Marsh Wildlife Sanctuary: A 300-acre coastal freshwater wetland with scenic walking trails, diverse bird species, and educational programs for nature enthusiasts.
    • UCI Arboretum: A tranquil 12.5-acre botanical garden featuring unique and rare plant species cultivated by the University of California, Irvine.
    • Sherman Library & Gardens: A peaceful horticultural retreat showcasing themed gardens, exotic plant collections, and a historical research library.
    • Upper Newport Bay Nature Preserve: A vast coastal wetland known for its serene hiking trails, scenic overlooks, and opportunities for birdwatching and kayaking.
  • Beaches and Outdoor Activities
    • Newport Beach: A picturesque beach destination known for its golden sands, excellent surfing conditions, luxury waterfront homes, and lively harbor area.
    • Balboa Island: A charming island community accessible by ferry, featuring quaint shops, delicious frozen bananas, and stunning waterfront views.
    • Corona Del Mar State Beach: A scenic and family-friendly beach ideal for sunbathing, swimming, tidepool exploration, and enjoying breathtaking coastal views.
