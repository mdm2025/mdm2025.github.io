Brussels, Belgium

IEEE MDM 2024: Call for Industry and Application Track Papers

Join us at IEEE MDM 2024’s Industry, Systems and Application track! Continuing its history, MDM 2024 Industry, Systems and Application Track solicits submissions of pioneering papers showcasing original contributions in developing and applying mobile computing and data management technologies towards the myriad of real-world problems.

We encourage both industrial and academic researchers and practitioners to delve into the intricacies of complex systems, share and discuss the challenges and solutions in complex real-world systems, as well as the practices and examples in commercial use cases.

Topics of Interest

We welcome submissions on the following topics - but not limited to:

• Theoretical Foundations of Data-intensive Mobile Computing
• Data Sensing Techniques in Mobile Computing
• Mobile Crowd-Sourcing and Crowd-Sensing
• Mobile Data Governance and Cleaning
• Mobile Data Management and Applications
• Data Security and Privacy in Mobile Data Computing
• Machine Learning/AI for Mobile Data
• Mobile Data Visualization Systems and Applications
• Novel Mobile Data Processing Tools/Middleware
• Location-Based Services and Applications
• Urban Computing Applications
• Contact tracing and object tracking
• Spatio-temporal sensor data processing

Submission Guidelines

• Page limit: 8 pages, including all figures, tables, and references.
• Submit your paper via: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/MDM2024/
• All submissions need to follow IEEE Computer Society Proceedings Manuscript Formatting Guidelines. See templates here: https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html.

Note that a paper exceeding the page limit in the respective category may be rejected without review. Any appendices should remain within the page limit.

Submission of a meaningful abstract by the abstract deadline is a precondition for paper submission.

The Review Process will be single-blind (i.e., the reviewers are not identified, while author names are listed in the submission).
